Masonry

A curated collage of pictures that represent community in Kitchener-Waterloo and Huron Heights.

Hover over each image to read what emotions the author sees in their contribution. We have tried to represent each image without using the word community so that the readers can imagine themselves apart the scene.

Each picture with its description is just one brick in the beautifully crafted community of Kitchener-Waterloo. The site is called Masonry for that reason in the way each tile is weaved together like a brick wall.

Ethan Huber 2024
